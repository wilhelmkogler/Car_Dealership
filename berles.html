    Car Rental Process

      Selecting a Car:

    • Browse our currently available rental cars.

    • Choose the one that best suits your needs.

    • Check the availability of your selected car on the contact page.

      • Paying the Deposit:

    • Pay a deposit upon pickup to secure the rental.

    • The deposit amount: €500.

    • The deposit can be paid in cash or by credit card.

      • Picking Up the Car:

    • Arrive at the designated rental location to pick up the car.

    • Inspect the car for any damages or defects and document them in the rental agreement.

      • Getting Insurance:

    • Purchase insurance to cover any unexpected incidents or damages.

    • Insurance cost: €30/day.

    • Insurance must be arranged before signing the rental agreement.

      • Returning the Car:

    • Return the car to the specified location at the end of the rental period.

    • Inspect the car and compare its condition to when you received it, ensuring there are no damages.

    • If everything is in order, your deposit will be refunded upon return.
